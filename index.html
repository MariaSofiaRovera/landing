Hello everyone! My name is Maria Sofia, I am from Turin, Italy, and I give ski lessons. Just to give you a quick overview, I have started skiing 15 years ago and I am a ski instructor since 2021 so I am used to work in various situations and all my clients are very satisfied! I offer ski lessons for every levels and ages, and I can even prep you if you plan on competing. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie seeking thrills or a nature lover in search of peace and serenity, my ski lessons are designed to cater to all your expectations, so join the adventure!

Discovery lessons

Book this class if you have never skied before. If you have already taken more than 3 lessons, book the beginner one.

Beginners' lessons

In this class, I will help you to better control your speed and turn so that you feel more comfortable.

Seasoned skiers

If you regularly ski during your holidays, book this class. It won't be for beginners but we will keep a steady pace for you to follow, and I will show you some hidden spots.

Advanced skiers

This class is made for you if you want to experience the thrill of speeding down the slopes and off-piste skiing. I will ask you for your level before confirming your reservation as this class is not dedicated to improve your skiing skills.

Expert skiers

Book this class if you want to compete. We will discuss about your goals and will elaborate a plan to improve your performances.

Group lessons

If you want to take ski lessons as a group (min. 8 people, max. 15) you can book this lesson. This way, you can keep the ski adventure more intimate, with your friends, family, or even for a school trip.
